H O V E R I N G

"Every act of creation is first an act of destruction." - Pablo Picasso









It is widely theorized that the oceans of our planet arrived when comets collided with our planet, bringing water and life from all parts of the universe. These hellish and cataclysmic events became the essential foundation in our planets now vibrant existence.

In this film, 'Hovering,' the viewer explores a comets inspiring abilities to be both a creator and exterminator of life.
