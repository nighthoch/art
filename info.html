night·hoch /ˈnīt-hawk / noun

The nighthoch is a noctural organism and a local inhabitant of the West Coast, United States. Utilizing photosynthesis,
the nighthoch converts blue LED wavelengths into chemical energy, which can later be released to fuel the organism's metabolic activities.
